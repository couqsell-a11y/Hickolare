What is VOIP?

Voice over IP (VOIP) replaces traditional telephony by routing calls over the internet. This brings higher call quality, increased flexibility, and significantly lower costs. You can make and receive calls via landlines, computers, smartphones, or softphones—anywhere you have internet access.

Smart and Flexible Communication

All your telephony services are centralized in the cloud. Phone numbers are no longer tied to a physical location, allowing employees to stay reachable on their internal number—whether they’re at the office, working from home, or on the road. This cuts costs and speeds up internal communication.
